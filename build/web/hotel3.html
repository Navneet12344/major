About this hotel

A 10-minute walk from Raikabag Palace Junction train station, this unfussy budget hotel is 4 km from Sardar Market and 5 km from Mehrangarh, a history museum in a former 15th-century fortress. Basic, air-conditioned rooms with wood furnishings feature Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, as well as en suite wet rooms. Amenities include a restaurant and a bar. Breakfast and parking are available.
